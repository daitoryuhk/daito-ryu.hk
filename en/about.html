About

The Hong Kong Daito-ryu Aikijujutsu Association is authorized by Kondo Katsuyuki Sensei(Director of Daito-ryu Aikijujutsu).

Please visit ‘location’ on the official webpage of our HQ and click on ‘Asia’.

Dermot Creagh sensei holds a branch chief licence and a 3rd dan rank in Daito-ryu Aikijujutsu from Kondo Katsuyuki Sensei in 2006. Creagh sensei was the first non-Japanese student of Kondo sensei to receive a Branch Chief license (Shibucho) to teach the art outside of Japan.

We welcome male and female members above 18 who are mature and willing to make a long term commitment to learning the art
